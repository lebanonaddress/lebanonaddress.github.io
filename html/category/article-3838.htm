Note: The latest stable version is the 3.0 series. Also available is the 1.1.1 series which is our Long Term Support (LTS) version, supported until 11th September 2023. All older versions (including 1.1.0, 1.0.2, 1.0.0 and 0.9.8) are now out of support and should not be used.
